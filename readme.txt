Bookingmaster is a powerful plugin for managing bookings and reservations for your any Services, events, classes, or appointments.

== Description ==
Bookingmaster makes it easy to manage bookings and reservations for any type of services or appointment, whether it's a fitness class, workshop, car wash, repair service or private consultation.
With Bookingmaster, you can create booking solution with custom dates, times and allow users to book and pay for their spot using popular payment gateways available in WooCommerce.
Bookingmaster also includes a robust calendar system, making it easy to keep track of all your upcoming bookings.


Services that can be scheduled and booked online using BookingMaster include:
* **Repair Service** ( Car repair & maintanance, Television, Mobile Laptop Service Booking),
* **Cleaning and Wash Service** ( any kinds of cleaning service like car wash, House cleaning etc),
* **Salons & Beauty** (hair salons, nail salons and  barbershops),
* **Health and Wellness** (wellness, spas & massage, therapy, baths),
* **Medicine** (clinics, doctors, dentists, medical centers),
* **Education** (education centers, tutors, language schools, private lessons),
* **Fitness & Gyms** (yoga classes, personal trainers, health clubs, fitness centers) and
* **Professional Services** (coaches, consultants , lawyers , photographers , consulting psychologists , cleaning services) and many more.


### Who can benefit from using BookingMaster
Local businesses and individual providers who offer services to customers can benefit from using BookingMaster for WordPress since the booking form can be customized and personalized.
Many business sectors such as Cleaning & Wash Service, Repair Service, Paint Service, Healthcare, Beauty, Education, Medicine, Consulting have successfully implemented this solution and individuals have also taken advantage of it.
Additionally, web studios and developers looking to improve the appointment process for their customers will find BookingMaster an excellent solution.

== Installation ==

Upload the bookingmaster folder to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
Navigate to the Bookingmaster settings page to configure your options

= Q.I installed correctly but 404 error what can I do ?  =
A. You need to Re-save permalink settings it will solve the 404. if still not work that means you permalink not working, you may have httaccess problem or you have server permission problem.

= Q. What is Shortcode to display booking section  =
A. Here is shortcode  [bookingmaster post_id='id'] you can to display booking section any page.


= Q.Does Bookingmaster integrate with popular payment gateways? =
A. Yes, Bookingmaster includes integrations all payment method with woocommerce.

= Can I create custom events with Bookingmaster? =
A. Yes, you can create events with custom dates, times and set prices and availability for each booking

= Q.How does the calendar system work in Bookingmaster? =
A. The calendar system allows you to view all your upcoming events and bookings in a visual calendar format. You can view your calendar by day, week, or month, and easily manage your bookings functionality.
